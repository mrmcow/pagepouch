## ðŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### "URL is not on Google"

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 24-48 hours after submission
2. Check sitemap is properly formatted
3. Ensure no robots.txt blocking
4. Verify no noindex tags
5. Re-submit URL via URL Inspection

---

### "Crawled - currently not indexed"

**Why:**
- Google sees page but not prioritizing yet
- Needs more time or stronger signals

**Solutions:**
1. Add more internal links
2. Get social shares/backlinks
3. Wait (can take 2-4 weeks for new content)
4. Ensure content is high quality (you're good here)

---

### Low impressions after 2 weeks

**Check:**
1. Are pages indexed? (`site:` search)
2. Titles/descriptions compelling?
3. Target keywords too competitive?
4. Need more internal linking?

**Solutions:**
1. Optimize titles for clarity + keywords
2. Target long-tail keywords
3. Build more internal links
4. Get backlinks from relevant sites
