## Executive summary

Review text repeatedly surfaces **high-intent queries** that are only partly “Obsidian brand” queries—they are really about **web → Markdown**, **read-it-later replacement**, **AI chat archiving**, **citations / research**, and **bulk capture**. Many users praise **templates, reader mode, and highlights**; detractors spike on **Markdown quality**, **social sites**, **multi-tab/batch**, and **local integration fragility**.

**SEO opportunity:** Publish a **cluster** of pages and long-form posts that answer these intents **without** pretending to be Obsidian—instead positioning PageStash as **capture + cloud archive + export (.md, .html, .json, .csv, citations)** with a **clear Obsidian import path**.

## Karpathy & Markdown: content thesis (compliant framing)

Reviews show **non-Obsidian** install motivation tied to a **public figure + Markdown/PKM culture**. For SEO and trust:

1. **Do not** over-attribute private advice—frame as **“a common 2024–2026 onboarding path”** or **“widely discussed Markdown-first research stacks”** unless you have a **primary source link** to quote.
2. **Do** target the **user job**: “I heard credible people organize research in Markdown—**what clipper do I use?**”
3. **Bridge:** PageStash answers **capture + provenance + export**; Obsidian (or Zed, VS Code, Typora, etc.) answers **thinking and linking**.

**Suggested pillar title patterns (A/B test in Search Console):**

- “Markdown research stacks: why **capture** belongs in the browser, not the notes app”
- “**Web clipper that exports clean Markdown** (and when to use HTML + screenshots instead)”
- “**Obsidian optional**: build a web research archive that still exports to `.md`”

**Primary keywords to instrument:** `web clipper markdown`, `save webpage as markdown`, `export chatgpt to markdown`, `obsidian web clipper alternative`, `markdown research workflow`, `clip articles to markdown`.

## Risks & guardrails

- **Trademark:** Use “Obsidian” factually (compatibility); don’t imply endorsement.
- **Person mentions:** Karpathy-related content should be **workflow/education**, not celebrity clickbait, unless citing public posts with links.
- **Comparisons:** Link to competitor **docs** or store listing when stating their features; avoid stale specifics (extension version churn).
